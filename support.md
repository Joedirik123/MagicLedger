# Magic Ledger Support

Magic Ledger is a personal portfolio tracker for Magic: The Gathering collectors. This page is the support contact and a quick FAQ.

## Contact

For bug reports, feature requests, or questions, email **joedirik77@gmail.com**. I read every message.

## FAQ

**Where is my data stored?**
On your device. If iCloud is enabled, it also syncs to your private iCloud container — Apple's encrypted, per-user storage. No one but you can read it.

**Why are some of my prices missing?**
Scryfall doesn't always have a price for every printing or finish. The price will appear automatically once Scryfall publishes one.

**How often do prices refresh?**
Once every 12 hours automatically, or any time you pull to refresh on the Dashboard.

**Can I export my collection?**
Yes — Inventory tab → toolbar menu → Export CSV. You can also export per-binder.

**The app keeps asking for camera permission.**
The card scanner uses the camera to read card titles. If you've denied permission, re-enable it in Settings → Magic Ledger → Camera.

**A widget shows placeholder values (Lightning Bolt, Counterspell, Black Lotus).**
Open the main app once and refresh prices. The widget reads a snapshot the app writes after each refresh.
