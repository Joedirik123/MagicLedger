# Magic Ledger Privacy Policy

Effective: May 1, 2026

Magic Ledger is designed to operate entirely on your device.

**What we collect:** Nothing. Magic Ledger has no user accounts and
sends no analytics, telemetry, crash reports, or behavioral data
anywhere.

**What stays on your device:** Your inventory, binders, decks,
wishlist, price alerts, and transaction log.

**iCloud sync (optional):** If you have iCloud enabled, your
collection data syncs across your own Apple devices via Apple's
private CloudKit container. Apple cannot read this data and we
cannot read this data.

**Third-party data sources:** Magic Ledger fetches card metadata and
pricing from Scryfall's public API (https://scryfall.com). Requests
identify only the app and contain no personal information about you.

**Notifications:** If you enable price alerts, Magic Ledger schedules
local notifications on your device. No notification data is sent
externally.

**Camera:** The card scanner uses the camera locally to read card
titles. No images are uploaded.
